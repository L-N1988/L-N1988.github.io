1 % This script processes and visualizes Power Spectral Density (PSD) data 
 2 % from a given dataset. It generates multiple plots to analyze the PSD 
 3 % and pre-multiplied PSD at different vertical positions in the dataset. 
 4 % Additionally, it creates a contour plot to visualize the variation of 
 5 % pre-multiplied PSD along the vertical centerline.
 6 
 7 % The script performs the following steps:
 8 % 1. Loads PSD data (`pxxs`) and frequency data (`fs`) from a specified file.
 9 % 2. Extracts data at specific vertical positions (1/4, 1/2, and 3/4 of the 
10 %    vertical range) and plots:
11 %    - PSD vs frequency on a log-log scale.
12 %    - Pre-multiplied PSD (`f * PSD`) vs frequency on a log scale.
13 % 3. Extracts PSD data along the vertical centerline and interpolates it 
14 %    onto a finer grid for visualization.
15 % 4. Generates a contour plot of the pre-multiplied PSD along the vertical 
16 %    centerline, with frequency on the x-axis and vertical position on the 
17 %    y-axis.
18 
19 % Key variables:
20 % - `fs`: Frequency data (Hz).
21 % - `pxxs`: PSD data (m^2/s).
22 % - `Y`: Vertical positions (m).
23 % - `ymesh`: Vertical grid positions (m).
24 % - `xv`, `yv`, `vv`: Data for interpolation (frequency, vertical position, 
25 %   and pre-multiplied PSD, respectively).
26 % - `xq`, `yq`, `vq`: Interpolated grid data for contour plotting.
27 
28 % Notes:
29 % - The script uses logarithmic scaling for frequency and PSD axes to 
30 %   better visualize data spanning multiple orders of magnitude.
31 % - The contour plot provides a detailed view of how the pre-multiplied 
32 %   PSD varies with frequency and vertical position.
33 % - Ensure that the required data files are available in the specified 
34 %   `casePath` directory before running the script.
35 
36 load(fullfile(casePath, 'figure_data', 'pxxs.mat'));

38 [ii_c, jj_c] = deal(round(size(fs, 1)/4), round(size(fs, 2)/4));
39 figure();
40 plot(reshape(fs(ii_c, jj_c, :), [], 1), reshape(pxxs(ii_c, jj_c, :), [], 1));
41 grid on; set(gca, 'XScale', 'log'); set(gca, 'YScale', 'log');
42 set(xlabel("$f$ (Hz)", "FontSize", 14), 'Interpreter', 'latex');
43 set(ylabel("$S_{uu}(f) (m^2/s)$", "FontSize", 14), 'Interpreter', 'latex');
44 set(title(sprintf("PSD at y = %.5f m", Y(jj_c)), FontSize=14), 'Interpreter', 'latex');
